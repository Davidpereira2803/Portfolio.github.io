Who am I?

If you came this far you are probably wondering who I am, well I am David a 20 year old Computer Science student, who is deeply interested in programming and IT in general.
Currently I am a second year Computer Science student at the University of Luxembourg and I am preparing to later on become a Software Engeneer.

What Projects have I contributed to?

During my studies I have worked with the Automation and Robotics Research Group (ARG), to do my Bachelor Semester Project.
In my second semester (BSPS2) I have worked with a DJI Tello drone and ORBSLAM3 to create maps of environments using the drone. I created a benchmark program to compare different sensors in different environments.
For my third semester I have worked again with ARG, but this time I contributed to a project of them, where I worked in a ROS environment to create a Behavior Tree to control different drone capabilities.

These Projects gave me the possibility to work with robots and discover the world where everything should work but nothing does work.
